THE SEVEN KANDAS

The Ramayan written by Tulsidas known as Ramcharitmanas is the sixteen century epics. The story revolves around Ramawho was the seventh avatar of the Hindu godVishnu. He is the central figure of the Hindu epic Ramayan, which the principal narration of the events cisonnected to His incarnation on earth. His ideals and His greatness. He was the eldest son of King Dashratha, the ruler of the Kosala Kingdom. Rama's life and the journey is one of adherence to dharma despite harsh tests and obstacles and many pains of life and time. This adherence made him earn the name of Maryada Purushottam which meansThe Perfect Man or Lord of self-controlLord of Virtue. For the sake of his father's honor, Rama abandons his claim to Ayodhya's throne to serve an exile of fourteen years in the forest. His wife Sita and brother Lakshmana decide to join him, and all three spend fourteen years in exile together. While in exile Sita is kidnapped by Ravana, the king of Lanka. After a long and arduous search, Rama fights a colossal war against Ravana's armies. In the war of powerful and magical beings, greatly destructive weaponry and battles. Rama slays Ravana in the battle and liberates his wife. Having completed his exile. Rama returns to be crowned king in Ayodhya and eventually becomes the emperor. The whole story is divided into seven parts known as 'Kanda'.
